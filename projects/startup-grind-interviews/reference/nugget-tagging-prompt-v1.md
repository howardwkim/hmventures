- opportunity_signal: pain-point/insider-knowledge framed around customer-discovery thinking —
  would this reveal something buildable/solvable for this person's business? Operational,
  product, and customer pain points at any company scale are in scope. Excludes
  fundraising/financing/deal-and-equity-structure content broadly — not just VC pitch/valuation
  mechanics, but anything about how a company is capitalized or how ownership is
  structured/documented: debt vs. equity choice, LLC vs. C-corp, revenue-share/profit-share
  models, cap tables, stock options, vesting, board-approval paperwork for equity.
- small_business_focus: independent tag, not a filter — true when the nugget's subject or advice
  is specifically about, or directly usable by, a small/bootstrapped/lifestyle business.
