# MiHO Partners — Brand DNA

## One-Line Positioning
Practical tools and real-talk content for small business owners doing $1M–$3M who are tired of generic advice and need someone who has actually run businesses — not a coach, a peer.

## Category and Real Competitors
**Category:** SMB consulting and content

**Real competitors:**
- Generic business coaches and LinkedIn thought leaders who theorize without operating experience
- Business content farms (Inc., Entrepreneur magazine) — useful but impersonal, no skin in the game
- Agency consultants who charge enterprise rates for cookie-cutter frameworks
- AI tools that give the same advice to everyone regardless of business type or stage

**How MiHO wins:** Mike has lived this. He's not pattern-matching from case studies — he's pattern-matching from real businesses he has built, advised, and watched fail. The credibility is the product.

## Core Mechanism — Why It Works
Most small business owners at $1M–$3M have figured out how to make money. What they haven't figured out is how to stop trading time for it. MiHO's mechanism is identifying the specific activity or system that, when fixed, unlocks the next stage — not a 12-week framework, one specific thing this week.

The content works because Mike takes a clear side. He doesn't hedge. Readers share it because they've finally heard someone say what they actually think, not what consultants say to cover themselves.

- **Content:** Free (blog at michaelgrabham.com, LinkedIn) as the top-of-funnel and credibility engine
- **Viral / StaffWriter tools:** Being productized as free tools to attract SMB clients (MiHO's product-led growth strategy)

## Hard Rules

**Claims you can make:**
- "I've seen this pattern in dozens of businesses"
- "Here's what actually works based on what I've observed"
- "Most owners I talk to..."

**Claims you cannot make:**
- Revenue guarantees or specific ROI promises
- "This will work for every business" — always acknowledge it depends on the type and stage
- Credentials you don't have (MBA, CPA, licensed financial advisor)

**Words you never use:**
- Synergy, leverage (as a verb), pivot, scale (as a verb used solo), ecosystem, holistic, empower, unlock potential, game-changer, thought leader, guru, crush it, 10x, hack
- "In today's fast-paced world" or any variant
- Em dashes (—) — strongest signal of AI-written content, kills credibility instantly
